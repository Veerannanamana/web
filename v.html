About B.Tech

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a four-year undergraduate engineering degree program. It provides students with a strong foundation in engineering principles and practical skills. B.Tech is offered in various branches, allowing students to specialize in specific areas of engineering.

Branches

  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

  • Artificial Intelligence

  • Data Dcience

  • Cyber Security

EMCET

Engineering, Medical, and Common Entrance Test (EMCET) is an entrance exam for admission into engineering and medical colleges in the state. It is a crucial step for B.Tech aspirants.



ECET

Engineering Common Entrance Test (ECET) is conducted for diploma holders and B.Sc (Mathematics) graduates to get lateral entry into B.Tech programs. It is a pathway for non-engineering graduates.
